Welcome to DFW Gaming Village

DFW Gaming Village is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to building community through tabletop gaming.

We host gaming and social events across North Texas and provide a traveling board game library for our members, local organizations, and conventions. As a charitable nonprofit, our mission is to promote social connection, education, and accessible gaming experiences for everyone.
